about us

start your journey with us

start your journey to a better life with our courses

VIJAYSHREE EDUCATION was created in 2018 by the vision and toil of Mr. Vishal Kumar Singh, a Mechanical Engineering Graduate from IIT Delhi. We had a very humble beginning as a forum for 11th , 12th and IIT-JEE, with a vision to provide an ideal launch pad for aspirants.

From school teachers and tutors to home schoolers and parents, engaged adults are the key to unlocking each child's potential and drive to learn. We empower all kinds of educators to teach kids by providing the best educational resources in any form or device to be used at home, at school, and everywhere in-between.
